ℹ️ About Us

Blockchain-based Decentralized Fruit Marketplace

🍎 What is Smart Fruit Store?

Smart Fruit Store is a decentralized fruit trading platform built on the Base Sepolia testnet. We leverage blockchain technology to enable transparent, secure transactions without traditional intermediaries.

Users can purchase fresh fruits using FRT tokens. All transaction records are permanently stored on the blockchain, immutable and verifiable at any time.

⛽ Gas Fee Explanation

Each transaction includes a 10% gas fee to cover blockchain network costs.

💡 Why Gas Fees?

Blockchain transactions require miners/validators to process. Gas fees compensate for network resource usage, ensuring transactions are quickly confirmed and recorded on the blockchain.

Gas Fee Calculation: Subtotal × 10% = Gas Fee

Example: Purchasing 10 FRT worth of fruits incurs a 1 FRT gas fee, totaling 11 FRT.

💳 Payment Process

This demo uses simulated payments - no real wallet or cryptocurrency required. Clicking "Confirm Payment" simulates a complete blockchain transaction流程:

  1. ✓ Validate Order - Check items and prices
  2. ✓ Reduce Stock - Update inventory quantities
  3. ✓ Generate TX Hash - Create unique transaction ID
  4. ✓ Blockchain Confirmation - Simulate network confirmation

The entire process takes approximately 3-4 seconds, after which you'll see a transaction hash and order details.
